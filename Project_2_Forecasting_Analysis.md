## CFO Recommendations

3. **Adopt a weekly budget-versus-actual cadence.** Have each category owner (Marketing, Operations, Payroll, Software) submit a mid-month variance report. Flag any category tracking more than 3% over budget by week 2 so corrective actions—reallocations or spend freezes—can be taken before month-end close.
